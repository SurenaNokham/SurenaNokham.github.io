Professional Background

I am currently the Analyst Data for the R&D Data Management Team at Tyson Foods Inc., where my primary responsibility is overseeing the migration of legacy Hillshire plants into Tyson's sophisticated Labeling System. As the Subject Matter Expert for Tyson Labeling System (TLS) and Tyson Label Generator (TLG), my role encompasses a broad spectrum of technical and operational functions. These include the creation, maintenance, and updating of label templates using Adobe Illustrator, a tool in which I have gained considerable proficiency. Additionally, I ensure the efficient distribution of labels to plants via TLG upon request and act as the emergency contact for critical downtime issues, swiftly troubleshooting problems or coordinating with IT and services to restore operations. My responsibilities extend to managing offline projects that fall outside our Product Data Management (PDM) system, keeping meticulous track of inventory, and assisting R&D Scientists with technical issues related to Zebra printers. I also handle box office tickets from plants, resolving emergency incidents and overseeing cost accounting requests to facilitate material management and specification changes within our PDM system.

Prior to my current role, my career trajectory at Tyson Foods included significant stints as a Senior Research Laboratory Microbiologist, an Associate Laboratory Microbiologist, and a Laboratory Analyst for IEH Laboratories. In these capacities, I honed my skills in executing non-routine processes, conducting comprehensive studies, and standardizing procedures to adhere to food safety standards. My experiences have imbued me with a robust understanding of microbial behaviors, enhancing my capability to lead and streamline workflows while boosting operational efficiency across teams.
